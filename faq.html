Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to join?

No experience is needed! Everyone is welcome to join and learn.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership is free for all students of the university.

How often are events held?

We organize at least one event every month, including indoor and outdoor activities.

Can I suggest new activities?

Yes! We always welcome new ideas from members. You can contact the committee.
